## About Me

I am Aman Sheikh, a Flutter developer from Kathmandu, Nepal, currently working at MarginTop Solutions. I focus on building cross-platform mobile apps with solid architecture, clean state management, robust API integration, and disciplined release workflows.

I am the kind of developer who cares about the invisible parts too: code structure, maintainability, testability, versioning, deployment hygiene, and the small technical decisions that decide whether an app survives production gracefully or becomes a maintenance headache later.

My work has covered exam preparation apps, sports e-learning products, medical workflows with BLE and mesh networking, and retailer-distributor management systems. Across those projects, I have worked on authentication, payments, Firebase, release management, QA support, and reusable boilerplate design.

In practice, I operate like a product engineer: I think in flows, failure modes, edge cases, and long-term maintainability, not just screens and widgets.

## Featured Projects

### Striide: The Girls Cricket App

A Flutter-based sports e-learning platform with daily structured training content, custom video and audio playback, Riverpod state management, testing, environment-based flavors, and GitHub Actions CI/CD.

### JFT & Skill Mock Test

A bilingual exam preparation app published on Google Play Store and App Store, featuring custom test systems, real-time result processing, Google and Apple Sign-In, eSewa and FonePay payment integration, Firebase services, and production release workflows.

### Sura Connect

A medical application featuring BLE and mesh networking for healthcare device communication, along with patient progress tracking, treatment history, data visualization, and codebase optimization.

### Boxtock

A retailer and distributor management app with API integrations, location-based features, OpenStreetMap support, reusable architecture, standardized themes, and modular structure for scalability.
